# Hall Booking System - User Manual
**For Staff & Administrators**

## 1. Getting Started
### Accessing the System
*   **Web Portal**: Go to [https://app.mthall.com](https://app.mthall.com)
*   **Mobile App**: Open the "Hall App" on your Android device.

### Login
1.  Enter your **Email Address**.
2.  Enter your **Password**.
3.  Click **Login**.

---

## 2. Dashboard
The **Dashboard** gives you a quick overview of the current month:
*   **Income**: Total money collected this month.
*   **Expense**: Total expenses recorded this month.
*   **Profit**: Income minus Expense.
*   **Upcoming Events**: Number of confirmed bookings for the future.

---

## 3. Managing Bookings (Events)
### Checking Availability
1.  Click on **Calendar** in the menu.
2.  Green slots are **Available**. Red slots are **Booked**.

### Creating a Booking
1.  Click **New Booking** (or "+" on Mobile).
2.  **Date & Time**: Select the Event Date and Hall.
3.  **Client Details**: Enter Name and Phone Number.
4.  **Payment**: Enter the "Advance Amount" received.
5.  Click **Save**. The slot will now turn Red.

---

## 4. Financials
### Adding Income
*   When a client pays the balance, go to **Incomes** > **Add Income**.
*   Select the **Event**, enter the **Amount**, and choose the **Payment Mode**:
    - **Cash** - Cash payments
    - **Card** - Credit/Debit card
    - **UPI** - UPI/Digital wallets
    - **Bank Transfer** - Bank transfers/NEFT/RTGS
    - **Cheque** - Cheque payments
    - **Other** - Any other payment method
*   Click **Save**.

### Recording Expenses
*   Go to **Expenses** > **Add Expense**.
*   Select a Category (e.g., Cleaning, Electricity) and enter the Amount.

---

## 5. Reports
*   Go to **Reports** to see monthly breakdowns.
*   **Note**: Income is calculated based on the **Booking Date** (when the event happens), giving you a clear picture of monthly revenue performance.

---

## 6. Admin Settings
### Creating Users
1.  Go to **Users**.
2.  Click **Add User**.
3.  Enter their Name, Email, and Role (Admin or Staff).
4.  **Assign Halls**: Select which halls this user can manage (required - at least one hall must be selected).
    - **Admin users** automatically have access to all halls
    - **Staff users** can only access the halls you assign to them
5.  Click **Save**.

### Managing User Hall Access
1.  Go to **Users**.
2.  Click **Edit** on a user.
3.  In the **Assigned Halls** dropdown, select or deselect halls.
4.  Click **Save**.

**Important Notes:**
- Users can only see and manage events/expenses for their assigned halls
- Users must be assigned to at least one hall
- Admin users always have access to all halls regardless of assignments

### Resetting Passwords
1.  Go to **Users**.
2.  Click **Edit** on a user.
3.  Enter the new password in the Password field.
4.  Click **Save**.
