# Hall Booking System - Target Market Research

## Ideal Customer Profile

### Primary Target: Religious & Community Centers
**Why they need this:**
- Manage multiple halls/rooms (prayer halls, dining halls, community spaces)
- Handle frequent bookings from members and external clients
- Track expenses (utilities, maintenance, catering)
- Need customizable branding per location
- Often run by volunteers who need simple, accessible tools

**Examples:**
- Churches, Temples, Mosques, Synagogues
- Community centers with event spaces
- Religious retreat centers

### Secondary Targets

#### 1. Educational Institutions
- **Schools & Colleges**: Auditoriums, gymnasiums, conference rooms
- **Universities**: Multiple venues across campus
- **Training Centers**: Classroom and seminar hall bookings

#### 2. Corporate Event Spaces
- **Co-working Spaces**: Meeting room management
- **Hotels**: Banquet halls and conference facilities
- **Convention Centers**: Multiple halls with complex scheduling

#### 3. Government & Municipal
- **Town Halls**: Public meeting spaces
- **Libraries**: Community rooms and event spaces
- **Recreation Centers**: Sports halls, activity rooms

#### 4. Private Event Venues
- **Banquet Halls**: Wedding and event bookings
- **Country Clubs**: Multiple facility management
- **Party Venues**: Birthday, corporate events

## Qualification Criteria

### High-Priority Prospects (Hot Leads)
- ✅ Manage 2+ halls/rooms
- ✅ Handle 10+ bookings per month
- ✅ Currently using manual methods (Excel, paper)
- ✅ Have website/online presence
- ✅ Charge for bookings (revenue-generating)

### Medium-Priority Prospects (Warm Leads)
- ⚠️ Single hall but high booking volume
- ⚠️ Using basic software (Google Calendar)
- ⚠️ Growing organization
- ⚠️ Mix of free and paid bookings

### Low-Priority Prospects (Cold Leads)
- ❌ Very low booking volume (<5/month)
- ❌ No budget for software
- ❌ Resistant to technology
- ❌ Single room with simple needs

## Competitive Analysis

### What They're Currently Using

#### Manual Methods
- **Excel/Google Sheets**: Error-prone, no automation
- **Paper Calendars**: No digital access
- **WhatsApp/Email**: Scattered communication

#### Basic Software
- **Google Calendar**: No payment tracking, limited features
- **Eventbrite**: High fees (3-5% + payment processing)
- **SimplyBook.me**: Generic, not hall-specific

#### Enterprise Solutions
- **EMS Software**: Expensive ($5,000+/year)
- **Momentus**: Complex, overkill for small venues
- **Planning Pod**: $40-$100/month but limited customization

### Your Competitive Advantages
✅ **Custom Branding**: Each client gets their own branded app
✅ **Affordable**: Lower cost than enterprise solutions
✅ **Simple**: Easier than complex enterprise tools
✅ **Mobile-First**: Android app included
✅ **Local Support**: Direct access to developer
